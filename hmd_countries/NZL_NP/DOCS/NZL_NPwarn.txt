Data for New Zealand are available for the national population as well as for the two main ethnic groups: Maori and Non-Maori. Click on the links below to enter the subpage that lists the data files for each of these populations.

The Background and Documentation (for National Population)  contains all of the documentation for the country as a whole as well as for ethnic subgroups. Background and Documentation for  Maori and  Non-Maori contain only information specific to those subgroups.
